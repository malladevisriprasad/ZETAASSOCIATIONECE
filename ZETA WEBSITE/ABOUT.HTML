About ZETA

ZETA Organization (Zone for Electronics Technologies and Automation) is a student-driven technical organization established under the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at BEHARA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY.

The organization aims to encourage innovation, technical excellence, leadership, teamwork, project development and industrial exposure among engineering students.

Objectives

  • Promote technical innovation.
  • Conduct workshops and seminars.
  • Encourage project development.
  • Develop leadership qualities.
  • Create industry-ready engineers.

Why ZETA?

ZETA provides a platform for students to improve technical knowledge, communication skills, project management and professional networking.
